# 🎮 Vault Rush - Player's Guide

## How to Play

### Starting a Run
1. Each run costs **1 Key**
2. You start with **3 Keys** and **1 Revive Token**
3. Click **"Start Run"** on the home screen

### During a Run
You'll face **5 vaults** in sequence. Each vault contains a random reward or trap.

**After opening each vault, you have 2 choices:**
1. **Bank** - Save your rewards and end the run
2. **Risk** - Continue to the next vault for bigger rewards

### Vault Rewards
- 💎 **Small Gems** - 3 gems × multiplier
- 💎💎 **Medium Gems** - 7 gems × multiplier
- 💎💎💎 **Big Gems** - 18 gems × multiplier
- ⚡ **Multiplier** - Increases your gem rewards by +0.5x
- 🔑 **Bonus Key** - Get an extra key
- ✨ **Cosmetic Shard** - Unlock cosmetics
- 💥 **Trap** - Lose all unbanked rewards!
- 🎰 **Jackpot** - 150 gems × multiplier

### The Risk Meter
- **Vault 1:** 2% trap chance (safest)
- **Vault 2:** 7% trap chance
- **Vault 3:** 6% trap chance
- **Vault 4:** 18% trap chance
- **Vault 5:** 38% trap chance (highest rewards!)

### If You Hit a Trap
1. **Use a Revive Token** - Continue the run (if you have one)
2. **Watch an Ad** - Get a free revive
3. **Abandon** - Lose all unbanked rewards

### Banking Rewards
- Gems are converted at 50% rate (to balance the economy)
- Daily events can boost this rate (up to +30% on Saturdays!)
- Subscription gives +10% bonus

## Progression System

### Leveling Up
- Earn **XP** from opening vaults and banking gems
- Each level requires more XP (exponential curve)
- **Level rewards:**
  - Gems: Level × 5
  - Keys: 1 + floor(Level / 10)
  - Shards: floor(Level / 8)

### Daily Rewards
- Claim **once every 24 hours**
- **Streak bonus:** Rewards scale with your streak
- **Rotation:** Gems → Keys → Revive Tokens
- Miss a day = streak resets!

### Daily Events
- **Sunday:** Streak Sunday (+15% XP)
- **Monday:** Key Rush Monday (+1 shard per vault)
- **Tuesday:** Shard Surge Tuesday (+1 shard per vault)
- **Wednesday:** Hump Day Boost (+10% gems on bank)
- **Thursday:** Lucky Thursday (+10% gems on bank)
- **Friday:** Frenzy Friday (+20% gems on bank)
- **Saturday:** Double Gems Saturday (+30% gems on bank)

## Cosmetics System

### How to Unlock
1. Collect **Cosmetic Shards** from vaults
2. Go to **Collection** screen
3. Unlock cosmetics with shards
4. **Equip** to activate bonuses

### Vault Skins (Gem Multipliers)
- **Golden Vault** (120 shards) - 3x gem drops
- **Crystal Vault** (200 shards) - 2x gems + jackpot boost
- **Obsidian Vault** (300 shards) - Safer runs + XP boost
- **Diamond Vault** (600 shards) - 4x gems + jackpot hunter

### Avatars (Odds Manipulation)
- **Vault King** (150 shards) - Higher jackpot chance
- **Shadow Opener** (150 shards) - More medium gems, fewer traps
- **High Roller** (350 shards) - Massive jackpot boost (risky!)
- **Gem Magnet** (250 shards) - Extra gems on every open

### Badge Frames (All-Around Bonuses)
- **Gold Frame** (80 shards) - Jackpot + XP boost
- **Diamond Frame** (200 shards) - Balanced bonuses
- **Platinum Frame** (400 shards) - Extra shards + fewer traps + more XP

### Stacking Bonuses
You can equip **one of each type** (Vault + Avatar + Badge) and their bonuses **stack**!

**Example combo:**
- Diamond Vault (4x gems)
- Gem Magnet (1.2x gems)
- Gold Frame (1x gems)
- **Total: 4.8x gem multiplier!**

## Responsible Play

### Virtual Currency Only
- All rewards are **virtual gems and cosmetics**
- **No cash value** - cannot be withdrawn or exchanged
- **No real-money gambling** - this is an arcade game!

### Fair Odds
- All probabilities are **visible** (tap info button during runs)
- Outcomes are **weighted random** (no manipulation)
- **No guaranteed wins** - every vault is a chance

### Take Breaks
- Runs are designed to be **short** (30-60 seconds)
- **No pressure** to spend real money
- **Free keys** available via ads
- Play for **fun**, not profit!

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more keys?**
A: Watch ads, buy with gems, level up, or claim daily rewards.

**Q: What happens if I close the app during a run?**
A: Your run is saved! Return to continue where you left off.

**Q: Do cosmetics really help?**
A: Yes! They provide real gameplay bonuses (gem multipliers, better odds, etc.)

**Q: Can I reset my progress?**
A: Clear your browser's LocalStorage to start fresh.

**Q: Is there a max level?**
A: No! The XP curve continues infinitely.

**Q: What's the best vault to bank at?**
A: Vault 3 is the sweet spot for consistent gains. Vault 5 is high risk/high reward.

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Yes! The game works offline (LocalStorage-based).

**Q: Will my progress sync across devices?**
A: Not yet - this is an MVP. Cloud saves coming in future updates!
